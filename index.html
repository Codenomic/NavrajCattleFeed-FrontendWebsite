NAVRAJ CATTLE FEED Usage Instructions

Important Instructions:

  1. For buffaloes, mix 1 kg of Navraj Feed for every 2 liters of milk produced.
  2. For cows, mix 1 kg of Navraj Feed for every 2.5 liters of milk produced.
  3. Mix Navraj Feed with the old feed for the first week and gradually increase the quantity.
  4. There is no need to moisten Navraj Feed.
  5. Once the animal starts consuming Navraj Feed fully, there is no need to add any kind of oilcake, mineral mix, oil, ghee, or jaggery.
